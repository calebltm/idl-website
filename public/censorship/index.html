Help stop government censorship forever!

Government censorship is out of control. The Turkish government recently blocked access to Twitter and YouTube. The UK is censoring any site that might have adult content. China and Iran censor huge portions of the Internet. This is a violation of human rights and a threat to the Internet as we know it — and we're fighting back!

Join the Internet Defense League's Anti-censorship Network by adding a few lines of code to your site. Then, whenever anyone visits from a country where the Internet is censored, we'll pop up an overlay showing them how to use anonymity and VPN software to get unrestricted access to the full Internet! Together we can make government censorship a thing of the past!

Get the code!

How it works

Embed the Anti-censorship Network code on your site, and whenever someone visits from a country with Internet censorship, we show them circumvention information hosted by our network of Mirror servers. The Network gets stronger every time someone joins with their site or hosts a Mirror server.

Calling all developers!

We need your help to run the Mirror servers that power the Anti-censorship Network. The more Mirrors that join the Network, the harder it is for governments to block (think Whack-a-Mole :)
