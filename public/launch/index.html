Cat spotlights are going to blaze across hot July night skies on July 19th at 9pm in San Francisco, New York, DC, and London. And there'll be a party on the decks below.

If you pitch in now, we can get these spotlights up in these cities, and possibly more. Then we can wait for the videos and pictures and smile at all the things the internet makes possible.

Most cities will shine a spotlighty (the 1000 watt kind) and get a laser projector to etch cat signals on nearby buildings. In megacities where this is possible, a mega spotlight will shine cat signals into the sky.

If you want to get a cat signal hovering in the clouds in your city, send us an email at team@fightforthefuture.org and we'll get you set up. If you are ready to go all in, donate $700 and we'll send you everything you need to get going.
